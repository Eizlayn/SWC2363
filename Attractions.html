Our Main Rides

Roller Coaster

A high-speed thrill ride with loops, twists, and drops that provides an adrenaline-pumping experience for thrill-seekers.

Requirements

  • Height: Minimum height requirement of 48 inches (122 cm).
  • Weight: No weight restrictions.
  • Age: Recommended for ages 10 and above.
  • Health: Guests with heart conditions, back problems, neck problems, motion sickness, or recent surgeries should avoid this ride. Pregnant women and guests with medical conditions that could be aggravated by sudden drops, high speeds, or strong forces should also refrain from riding.

Log Flume

A water ride featuring gentle slopes, thrilling drops, and splash zones as riders navigate through winding water channels in hollowed-out logs.

Requirements

  • Height: Minimum height requirement of 36 inches (91 cm) to ride with an adult; 42 inches (107 cm) to ride alone.
  • Weight: No weight restrictions.
  • Age: Suitable for all ages, but children under 7 years old must be accompanied by a responsible adult.
  • Health: Guests with heart conditions, back problems, or motion sickness should use caution. Pregnant women and guests with medical conditions that could be aggravated by water rides or sudden drops should refrain from riding.

Bumper Cars

A fun-filled attraction where visitors can bump and spin their way through an electrifying arena in colorful, bumper-equipped cars.

Requirements

  • Height: Minimum height requirement of 42 inches (107 cm) to ride alone; shorter guests may ride with a responsible adult.
  • Weight: No weight restrictions.
  • Age: Recommended for ages 6 and above.
  • Health: Guests with neck or back problems, heart conditions, motion sickness, or recent surgeries should use caution when participating. Pregnant women should avoid this ride.

Drop Tower

An exhilarating free-fall ride that takes passengers to the top of a towering structure before plunging them down at high speed, creating a thrilling sensation of weightlessness.

Requirements

  • Height: Minimum height requirement of 48 inches (122 cm).
  • Weight: Maximum weight limit of 300 lbs (136 kg).
  • Age: Recommended for ages 12 and above.
  • Health: Guests with heart conditions, back problems, or fear of heights should avoid this ride.

Giant Swing

A heart-pounding swing ride that propels riders high into the air, swinging them back and forth in a thrilling arc with breathtaking views of the park below.

Requirements

  • Height: Minimum height requirement of 48 inches (122 cm).
  • Weight: Maximum weight limit of 250 lbs (113 kg).
  • Age: Recommended for ages 10 and above.
  • Health: Guests with heart conditions, back problems, or motion sickness should use caution.

Spinning Coaster

A wild roller coaster experience with cars that spin independently, adding an extra dimension of excitement as riders navigate through twists, turns, and drops.

Requirements

  • Height: Minimum height requirement of 42 inches (107 cm).
  • Weight: Maximum weight limit of 300 lbs (136 kg).
  • Age: Recommended for ages 8 and above.
  • Health: Guests with motion sickness or back problems should use caution.

Our Main Attractions

Haunted Mansion

Descend into the eerie Haunted Mansion for a spine-chilling adventure through ghostly halls and mysterious chambers.

Requirements

  • Height: No height restrictions.
  • Age: Recommended for ages 8 and above due to spooky effects.
  • Health: Guests with heart conditions, claustrophobia, or sensitivity to flashing lights and loud noises should avoid this attraction.

Jungle Safari

An immersive adventure where visitors can explore lush landscapes, encounter animatronic wildlife, and learn about exotic plants.

Requirements

  • Height: No height restrictions.
  • Age: Suitable for all ages.
  • Health: Guests should be able to walk on uneven terrain and tolerate mild motion.

Space Odyssey

A futuristic journey through outer space featuring simulated spaceflight, interactive exhibits, and breathtaking views of distant galaxies.

Requirements

  • Height: No height restrictions.
  • Age: Recommended for ages 7 and above.
  • Health: Guests with motion sickness, fear of heights, or sensitivity to flashing lights should use caution.

Enchanted Castle

A magical castle experience complete with enchanting gardens, interactive storytelling, and a chance to meet beloved fairy tale characters.

Requirements

  • Height: No height restrictions.
  • Age: Suitable for all ages.
  • Health: Guests should be comfortable walking and standing for extended periods.

Pirate Cove

An adventure zone where visitors can embark on pirate-themed quests, explore hidden treasure caves, and witness thrilling pirate shows.

Requirements

  • Height: No height restrictions.
  • Age: Suitable for all ages.
  • Health: Guests should be able to navigate stairs and tolerate moderate motion.

Dino Discovery

An educational attraction showcasing lifelike animatronic dinosaurs, interactive fossil digs, and multimedia presentations on prehistoric life.

Requirements

  • Height: No height restrictions.
  • Age: Suitable for all ages.
  • Health: Guests should be comfortable walking and standing for extended periods.
